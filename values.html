THE ORGANIZATION'S CORE VALUES

  1. ACCOUNTABILITY

    Accountability is an assurance that an individual or organization is evaluated on its performance or behavior related to something for which it is responsible. The term is related to responsibility but is regarded more from the perspective of oversight.

  2. RESPECT

    At CEEU,we have positive esteem for all,whether disabled,poor or rich, we believe we are equal before are fore most creator.

  3. VOLUNTARISM

    We are freely willing to serve humanity for all causes,where in times of joy or sadness.

  4. TEAM WORK

    The act of being civilized and working together is termed as team work.At CEE,we partner with communities and other organisations to solve problems concerning humanity.

    We work hard until we meet our target goals and objectives.This is what we mean by being committed and CEE.
